So this is a project that I'm currently working on to learn a thing or two about 'practical' software development, since I always wanted to get all the skills required to be a 'Full Stack Developer'. Here in this space I'd put up design documents, key findings, or something interesting that I come across during the course of development.
### So what is this project about?
Well, ArthSaathi is your personal financial assistant, your friend who'll help you keep track of your day-to-day expenses, generate insights about where your money goes, and how you can save or invest your hard earned cash. 
### Sound familiar, is your idea really unique?
I don't think so. Maybe there are many apps/website that do the same, and maybe even better than what I'll be starting off with. But the reason why I'm working on this is simple: I want to learn. I want to expand my knowledge base. I want to understand how real apps work, end to end, from development to deployment.
### What are you planning to do?
Well, I'm planning to develop the ArthSaathi mobile app as well as the entire backend infrastructure required to make it work. I'll probably add some AI stuff too later on. I'm trying to keep things as much 'in-house' as possible in order to maximise my learning. In future if this gains traction and people start loving it, maybe I'll add more features on public demand.
### Cool, so what can I expect?
Stay tuned for further updates, I'll try to be as consistent as possible about the development. The entire project is contained in a monorepo on GitHub [here](https://github.com/MohitR1999/arthsaathi)
