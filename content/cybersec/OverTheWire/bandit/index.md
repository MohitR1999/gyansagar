---
title: Bandit wargame
---
The Bandit wargame is designed for beginners to get started with the challenges. It focuses on the basics of web exploits, linux commands and other stuff that can help you play different challenges. It is recommended to begin with this wargame.

The concept of the challenges under this wargame are pretty simple: Say you are on a level `X`, then you just need to go through the description of the level and find the way to get the password for the next level, `X + 1`.

In order to solve the challenges, you will need to connect to their server via SSH, details of which are as follows:
- Host: `bandit.labs.overthewire.org`
- Port: `2220`
